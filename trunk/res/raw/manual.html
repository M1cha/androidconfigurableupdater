1. Rom download

  • please download the Miui Pikachu Edition for Defy at http://bit.ly/MIUI-PE and move the Zip File into the "/miuiupdater" Folder on your SD Card ( if it isn't on your SD Card please create one )

2. Rom options

  • click on next at the StartScreen and choose your Rom
  • please have a look at the Changelog to know whats new
  • at the OptionScreen you can delete Apps or add Tweaks
  • with System/Data Apps just switch it "ON" to delete these apps
  • with Tweaks just switch it "ON" to install these tweaks
  • click on the end on "Save" to activate the Flags on your zipped Rom

3. Rom installation

  • now click on "Reboot" and your Smartphone will reboot automatically into ClockworkMod Recovery
  • here your can navigate with the "Volume Buttons" up and down and with the "Power Button" you confirm your choice
  • now go on -> install zip from sdcard, after that go on -> choose zip from sdcard and search for the Rom on your SD Card
  • confirm your choice with "Yes - Install PikachuEdition.zip"and wait until the Rom is installed
  • after everything is installed your Smartphone will reboot and MIUI will welcome you

Have FUN with it!!
